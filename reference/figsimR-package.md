# figsimR: Simulating Fig Wasp Community Assembly

Simulates the assembly and structure of fig wasp communities within fig
fruits (Ficus racemosa), capturing ecological processes such as entry
order, parasitism, spatial oviposition constraints, resource limitation,
and fruit abortion. Designed for ecological modeling and hypothesis
testing using empirical parameters. Current version 0.2.1 is integrated
with the "sink strength" theory. Added support for species-level
\`none\` entries in \`layer_preference\`, allowing some species to
bypass the ovary-layer preference filter. Clarified that
\`layer_preference\` refers to ovary-layer preference or accessibility.
Improved handling of user-defined fig-wasp communities or other species
interaction systems with custom species lists and species-specific
parameter objects. Retained \*Ficus racemosa\*-specific defaults for the
worked example while allowing users to override them with
system-specific parameters.
